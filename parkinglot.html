About

Smokie’s Diner is a classic comfort-food spot where hearty breakfasts and timeless diner favorites come together in a warm, welcoming setting. Known for fluffy pancakes, cheesy omelettes, and crispy hash browns, Smokie’s is the kind of place where breakfast cravings are always satisfied. The menu stretches well beyond the griddle, featuring crowd-pleasers like chicken and waffles, tuna melts, Philly cheesesteaks, club sandwiches, and soul-warming matzo ball soup. To top it all off, Smokie’s serves up indulgent desserts—from chocolate-covered cheesecake to peach cobbler, apple pie, and creamy banana pudding—making it the perfect stop for any meal of the day and a sweet finish every time.
